Seenapse is an inspiration engine. It will help you to come up with more and better ideas, faster.


You can use Seenapse when you're a bit stuck, or in a hurry, or simply when you want to benefit from diverse ways of looking at the creative problem you're working on.


Which is pretty much always, in our experience.


In Seenapse, people share their mental associations.


It's like brainstorming with the world.

How to use Seenapse


Using Seenapse is easy: if you are working with something related to, let's say, coffee, just type in "coffee" in the search box. Each of the results can lead you to interesting, divergent stuff that people like you from all over the world have seenapsed.


The most important thing to keep in mind is this: what you find in Seenapse won't be the solution to your problem, but it will inspire you to come up with it. So don't just look at the seenapses, go deeper and think how do they relate to the problem, and then the solution will pop up in your mind.
